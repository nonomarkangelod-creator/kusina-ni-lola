Our Story

A Taste of Home

Kusina ni Lola started in a small kitchen in Pampanga, where our founder's grandmother cooked for the whole barangay every Sunday. The recipes have not changed — only the kitchen got bigger.

Every dish is made from scratch using ingredients sourced from local Filipino farmers and markets. Good food starts long before it hits the pan.
